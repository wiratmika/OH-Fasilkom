WHAT IS OPEN HOUSE
FASILKOM?

Open House Fasilkom is an annual event where you can learn what Fasilkom UI has to offer both inside and outside the class. We invite you to take a journey of experiencing life at Fasilkom for a day. We would like to help you make the best decision possible for your higher education, so come and have fun!
